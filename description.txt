S&P500 is a free-float, capitalisation-weighted index of the top 500
publicly listed stocks in the US. The data has been recorded since 1870.

This data set contains:
	-Date
	-SP500
	-Dividend
	-Earnings
	-Consumer Price Index (CPI)
	-Long Interest Rate
	-Real Price
	-Real Dividend
	-Real Earnings
	-PE10
